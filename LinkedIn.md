### 2. Profile photo
- [ ] **Replace the current photo** (13+ years old — credibility issue for senior product leadership).

Photo guidance:
- **Recency**: within last 1–2 years
- **Framing**: head + shoulders, eyes roughly 1/3 from top, looking slightly off-camera or directly at camera
- **Expression**: warm/approachable beats stern. For product leaders, warmth signals collaboration; serious-mode reads as corporate-stiff
- **Lighting**: even, soft, daylight preferred. Avoid harsh shadows
- **Background**: simple, muted, slightly out of focus. Avoid busy interiors, party shots, vacation backdrops, group crops
- **Wardrobe**: plain shirt or knit, solid colour. No logos, no novelty patterns
- **Resolution**: at least 800×800px, square crop

Cheapest acceptable: phone camera, friend taking the shot, north-facing window for light, plain wall. 15 minutes.  
Better: a Stockholm headshot photographer (1,500–3,500 SEK, ~30 min). Worth it for a senior leader profile that's about to be one of your main visibility tools.
