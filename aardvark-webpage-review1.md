## Purpose and scope

This document is a self-contained brief for the developer/designer iterating on `aardvark.pm`. It captures the structural fixes, copy edits, and additions that should land in v2.

**Scope of this page:** Consulting and executive-level positioning only — Fractional CPO, Principal PM, AI-native operating-model work, advisory.

**Out of scope (lives on a separate site):** founder / builder / startup work via Aardvark Labs. Do not introduce founder/co-founder framing, building/labs/tinkering language, or "open to founding-team conversations" signals on this page. They will live on a separate Aardvark Labs site.
